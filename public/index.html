Bullfight Finder is the ultimate companion for exploring the captivating bullfighting tradition of Terceira Island. Discover a world of thrilling events and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage. Stay up-to-date with upcoming bullfights, explore local traditions, and create unforgettable memories. Experience the adrenaline, excitement, and vibrant atmosphere of Terceira's bullfights like never before with Bullfight Finder. Unleash your adventurous spirit and embark on a journey of cultural discovery. Download now and let the adventure begin!

Discover the captivating bullfighting tradition of Terceira Island with Bullfight Finder. This app is designed to enhance your experience and provide you with essential information about bullfights happening in the area. Here are the key features that make Bullfight Finder your ultimate bullfighting companion:

Time & Location

Get accurate and up-to-date information about the time and location of each bullfight. Plan your schedule accordingly and ensure you're at the right place at the right time to enjoy the excitement.

Bullfight Radar

Using advanced location detection technology, Bullfight Finder's "bullfight radar" helps you discover nearby bullfight events. Stay informed about upcoming bullfights and never miss out on the action.

Bullfight Types

Learn about the different types of bullfights happening on Terceira Island. Whether it's a traditional or a special event, Bullfight Finder provides insights into the characteristics of each bullfight, enriching your cultural experience.

Local Business Support

Discover local businesses that actively support Bullfight Finder. These establishments provide valuable services and contribute to the preservation of the bullfighting tradition. Support them while enjoying your bullfighting adventure.

User-Friendly Interface

Bullfight Finder features a sleek and intuitive interface, making it easy to navigate and access all the app's functionalities. Enjoy a seamless user experience as you explore the world of bullfights.

Cultural Discovery

Immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Terceira Island's bullfighting tradition. Bullfight Finder is your gateway to experiencing the adrenaline, excitement, and vibrant atmosphere of bullfights while fostering a deeper understanding of the local culture.

Our Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Bullfight Finder Team ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the mobile application Bullfight Finder (the "App").

Overview

Bullfight Finder is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect and use information from our App.

Types of Data Collected

We collect the following types of information from users of our App:

  • information, such as usage data and device information, which is collected automatically when you use our App.

How Data is Used

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our App's functionality.
  • Analyse and improve our services, including to develop new features and services.

Third Party Sharing

We may share the information we collect with third-party service providers who help us to provide and improve our App. These may include analytics providers. The data shared with these providers is strictly limited to the information necessary to perform their services. Aditionally, they are prohibited from using it for any other purposes.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorised access, use, and disclosure. These measures include rules that deny or allow read or write access to the information our App provides. However, the information this app allows access to is provided by the City Halls of Angra do Heroísmo and Praia Da Vitória, and, as such, is publicly available.

Data Retention

We do not retain any user identifiable information, since the app is free and does not provide a login or other authentication method.

User Rights

To exercise your user rights, please contact us at bullfightfinder@gmail.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to the policy, we will notify you by email in the event that you have provided the address. Your continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your information, please contact us at bullfightfinder@gmail.com.
